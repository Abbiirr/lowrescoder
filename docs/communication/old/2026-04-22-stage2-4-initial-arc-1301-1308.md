Agent: Claude | Role: Reviewer/Architect | Layer: 1-2 | Context: TUI visual parity trigger documentation | Intent: Review tui-reference-scene-trigger-plan.md and tui-reference-scene-trigger-guide.md for honesty and consistency with the fresh matrix artifact

Verdict: **APPROVE with 3 notes**

## What I checked

Read `docs/tui-testing/tui-reference-scene-trigger-plan.md`, `docs/tui-testing/tui-reference-scene-trigger-guide.md`, and the fresh matrix `autocode/docs/qa/test-results/20260421-123752-tui-14-scene-capture-matrix.md` in full.

## Safety check: no scene overstated as triggerable today

The critical check passes. Every scene that is `direct` in the guide (`ready`, `active`, `recovery`, `sessions`, `palette`, `narrow`) is also `direct` in the fresh matrix. No blocked or partial scene is called out as a live capture anywhere in either document.

## Three notes

**1. Label drift: `review` and `cc` in the guide are still one position off.**

The guide has `04 Review` and `05 Command center`. The trigger plan correctly states the authoritative HTML ordering is `review=0005` and `cc=0006`. The guide has not been updated to match. This is exactly the drift the trigger plan called out in its Source Of Truth section. The guide needs a label-only correction to these two rows; the status and trigger columns are fine.

**2. Guide lists `plan`, `restore`, `escalation` as `blocked` with trigger "none" — but the fresh matrix has evidence triggers for all three.**

| Scene | Guide status | Guide trigger | Matrix status | Matrix trigger |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `plan` | `blocked` / "none" | | `partial` | `steps:[0.8, "/plan\r", 2.0]` |
| `restore` | `blocked` / "none" | | `partial` | `steps:[0.8, "__HALT_FAILURE__\r", 1.0]` |
| `escalation` | `blocked` / "none" | | `approximate` | `steps:[0.8, "__APPROVAL__\r", 1.0]` |

The guide is being more conservative than the matrix. That is safe (no overstatement), but it means the guide does not match the current capture tooling. A builder following the guide would think no trigger exists, even though `capture_reference_scene_matrix.py` successfully uses one.

Recommended fix: update the guide to say `partial` for `plan` and `restore`, `approximate` for `escalation`, and list the evidence triggers (with a note that they produce evidence-only captures, not promotable scenes).

**3. The trigger plan itself uses `blocked` for `plan` and `restore` while its own matrix column shows partial evidence triggers.**

The trigger plan's "Current truthful or evidence trigger" column correctly lists the triggers. But the "Current status" column says `blocked` for both. The fresh matrix says `partial`. These three columns are inconsistent within the plan doc itself.

Suggested fix: change the trigger plan's status column for `plan` and `restore` from `blocked` to `partial`, and `escalation` from `blocked` to `approximate` — matching both the matrix artifact and the trigger plan's own evidence-trigger entries.

## What is correct in both docs

- Mockup page ordering in the trigger plan is correct: `0001–0014` from the standalone HTML, with `review=0005` and `cc=0006` as authoritative.
- Dependency bundle groupings (A–F) are well-structured and match the execution order in `tui_implementation_plan.md`.
- Definition of Done (5 criteria) is correct and tight.
- Every `direct` scene is accurately listed as direct in both docs and the fresh matrix.
- The distinction between "current evidence trigger" and "planned canonical trigger" in the trigger plan is the right design.
